About The Elusive

The Elusive was founded at the beginning of Battle for Azeroth by Sin and Auraelias—who pugged most of Legion and realised how much they missed raiding. Sin and Aura are a married couple, but don't let that put you off.

Sin and Aura met on WoW during Wrath of the Lich King, and taught each other different aspects of the game. Many years later they formed a relationship, got married, and had a family. However, realising that they missed the game terribly, but didn't have a lot of time to play, they formed The Elusive as a relaxed community of raiders who enjoy playing the game without turning it into a part-time job.

As with a plethora of other guilds, Shadowlands caused our guild to take a bit of a hiatus, and we stopped raiding.

However, during the last patch of BfA, Sin and Aura also met Rayken, and later met him in real life, and realised that there was a close bond between us.

In the droughts following Shadowlands and Dragonflight, we discussed getting the guild back together to progress through content again, and were thrilled to find that many of our former raiders were interested in re-joining the guild and progressing with us, supporting our goals of having a strong community of like-minded individuals.

Even when WoW gets quiet, we have been known to explore other games together, and stay communicative with each other and support each other through every aspect of each other's lives.

We would love to have you join us and take our guild to new heights.

Recruitment

The Elusive is currently looking for skilled players to join our ranks. We're aiming to complete our roster to at least 20 players in order to start Mythic raiding. We're particularly interested in the following roles and specs:

  • Tanks: Protection Warrior, Protection Paladin, Vengeance Demon Hunter, Blood Death Knight
  • Healers: Preservation Evoker, Disc Priest, Holy Paladin
  • Melee DPS: Havoc Demon Hunter, Rogue (any spec), Retribution Paladin, Fury Warrior, Enhancement Shaman
  • Ranged DPS: Shadow Priest, Warlock (any spec), Fire Mage, Hunter (any spec), Balance Druid, Devastation Evoker
  • Support/Utility: Augmentation Evoker

If your class or spec isn’t listed here, don’t hesitate to apply — we’re open to all skilled and dedicated players who want to be part of a focused, mythic-ready team.

Requirements:

  • Consistent raid attendance (2 nights/week)
  • Willingness to learn and improve
  • Positive attitude and teamwork

We value performance over gear score. If you think you have what it takes, contact us!
