About The Elusive

Founded in the shadows of Azeroth, The Elusive is a guild of skilled adventurers who specialize in overcoming the most challenging content World of Warcraft has to offer.

We value teamwork, dedication, and a passion for the game. Our members come from all walks of life but share a common goal: to conquer every raid, dungeon, and challenge while maintaining a strong community spirit.

Whether you're a veteran player or new to the game, The Elusive offers a home for those who seek excellence in their WoW experience.

Recruitment

The Elusive is currently looking for skilled players to join our ranks. We're particularly interested in:

  • Exceptional Healers (Resto Druid, Holy Priest)
  • Skilled Tanks (Protection Paladin, Blood DK)
  • DPS with Mythic+ experience

Requirements:

  • Consistent raid attendance (3 nights/week)
  • Willingness to learn and improve
  • Positive attitude and teamwork
  • Properly geared for current content

We value performance over gear score. If you think you have what it takes, contact us!
